About Us

Welcome to IoT Alliance!

IoT alliance is a student club of SRM Institute of Science and Technology that revolves around the world of Embedded Systems and Internet of Things. Our team encourages research and product development, while reaching out to students through workshops and internships with the aim of sharing knowledge.
