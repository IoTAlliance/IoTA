About Us

Welcome to IoT Alliance

Internet of Things alliance is a student club of SRM Institute of Science and Technology that revolves around the world of Embedded Systems and Internet Of Things(IoT). The club specializes in creating projects, publishing research papers, conducting workshops and internships with the motive of sharing the knowledge.
